ABOUT LIFE ARMADA

Health Consultancy That Creates Wealth For Everyone

  • Who we Are?

    • We are a collective of healthcare providers in different capacities; ranging from nursing care pro-viders to medical doctors working with community organizers and hospitals all based in Nigeria.
    • We help our clients navigate the health care sector by organizing sustainable health awareness campaigns, and facilitating subscription health insurance schemes.
    • We are a force for good.

  • Our Primary Goal

    • To facilitate health awareness programs
    • To facilitate a health management organizations (HMO) enrollment scheme.

  • Our Place in the Healthcare Sector

    A major challenge in low income country like regarding healthcare provision is a paucity of infor-mation available to the public on how to access care in the opaque health health system. This also compounds the lack of information among the public required to manage their health. There is a pro-liferation of misinformations; a prevalence of half truth characterized by over spiritualized treatments and an overt dependence on tradition healing practices. We are actively involved in health awareness programs to address these deficiencies in health in-formation among the public.
